Bagel Pizza

Ingredients: 1. One original Bagel 2. One can Mariara 3. One cup Mozarella Cheese 4. One half cup Pepperoni

Instructions: 1. Layer marinara, cheese, and pepperoni on the bagel 2. Place in airfryer for fifteen minutes 3. Serve and Enjoy!

Slow Cooker Chili

Ingredients: 1. One pound Ground Beef 2. One can Tomatoes 3. One can Pinto Beans 4. One can Red Kidney Beans 5. Salt and Pepper 6. Your favorite packet of chili mix!

Instructions: 1. Put all ingredients in slow cooker 2. Set slow cooker to low 3. Cook for 6-8 hours 4. Add your favorite toppings such as cheese or avocado!

Sweet Chili Salmon

Ingredients: 1. Two filets of salmon 2. 1 tablespoon sriracha 3. 3 table spoons honey 4. 1 table spoon seasame oil 5. Salt and Pepper 6. 2 tablespooons of soy sauce

Instructions: 1. Mix sriracha, honey, seasame oil, soy sauce, and salt and pepper 2. Cut salmon and mix with mixture 3. Put in airfryer for 10 minutes or until desired cripsyness! 4. Serve with rice
